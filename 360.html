360-Degree Cameras

360-degree cameras capture everything around them in a full spherical view, offering immersive photo and video experiences. They are perfect for virtual reality content, real estate tours, and capturing unique perspectives.

Features of 360-Degree Cameras

Some key features of 360-degree cameras include:

  • Full spherical capture
  • High-resolution video and photo capabilities
  • Compact and portable design
  • Stitching software for seamless 360-degree images
  • Compatibility with VR headsets

Popular 360-Degree Camera Brands and Models

Some popular brands and models in the 360-degree camera market include:

  • Insta360 series (e.g., Insta360 ONE X2, Insta360 GO 2)
  • GoPro MAX
  • Ricoh Theta series (e.g., Ricoh Theta Z1, Ricoh Theta V)

360-degree cameras are excellent for creating immersive content that captures every angle of the scene, providing a unique and engaging experience.
